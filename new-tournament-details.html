Flawless Inferno League

Complete branding package for major esports tournament

Back to Tournaments

Tournament Overview

The Flawless Inferno League was a premier esports event featuring 32 top teams competing in Free Fire. The tournament ran for 3 weeks with a $5,000 prize pool.

My role was to create a complete visual identity that would work across all platforms including social media, streaming broadcasts, and venue signage.

May 10-30, 2023
32 Competing Teams
$5,000 Prize Pool
50,000+ Viewers

Design Concept

The design features fiery elements to represent the "Inferno" theme, with a color palette of deep reds, oranges and metallic accents.

The main poster uses dynamic composition to highlight key information while maintaining visual impact. All typography was carefully selected for maximum readability at various sizes.

The logo incorporates flame motifs forming an abstract "F" shape, representing both the tournament name and competitive spirit.

Deliverables

The complete package included:

  • Primary tournament logo (3 variations)
  • Main event poster (5 size variations)
  • Social media graphics kit
  • Stream overlays and transitions
  • Lower thirds and player cards
  • Venue signage and banners
  • Merchandise templates
