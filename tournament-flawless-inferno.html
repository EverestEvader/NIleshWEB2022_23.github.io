About the Tournament

The Flawless Inferno League was a premier esports competition featuring top teams from across the region. The tournament spanned over 6 weeks with intense matches broadcasted live to thousands of viewers.

My role was to create the complete visual identity including logo design, promotional materials, streaming assets, and social media graphics that would capture the fiery, competitive spirit of the event.

Flawless Esports

Logo Design

The logo was designed to embody the fiery competitive spirit of the tournament. The flame motif represents both the "Inferno" theme and the passion of esports competitors.

Key elements include:

  • Custom typography with sharp angles
  • Dynamic flame elements integrated with the letterforms
  • High-contrast color scheme for maximum visibility
  • Versatile design that works at all sizes

Promotional Materials

The tournament poster series was designed to build hype and communicate key information at a glance. The fiery visual theme was carried through all materials while maintaining clear hierarchy of information.

Materials created:

  • Main event poster
  • Social media banners and posts
  • Match overlay pack
  • Map Overlays
  • Road Map

Main Poster

The poster for the Free Fire Flawless Inferno League uses a bold red, black, and white color scheme with sharp typography to create a high-energy esports vibe. Key details like the NPR 5000 prize pool, free entry, and 312 slots are highlighted prominently, while flame-like accents reinforce the "Inferno" theme.

Key elements include:

  • Flawless Esports is prominently featured as the organizer, with their social handle.
  • Logos of the production and design partners are subtly placed at the bottom.
  • High-contrast color scheme for maximum visibility
  • Fitting for a competitive gaming event.
